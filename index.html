IN THE BLACK

// ORBITAL COMBAT //

Two ships. One shield. Shoot your opponent while the shield drifts between you. Don't let it pass you — or your enemy gets 2 seconds of cover. Best of 10 rounds. First to 6 wins.

P1: W/S or Mouse Wheel to move • Click or Space to fire
P2: Up/Down arrows to move • Enter to fire
